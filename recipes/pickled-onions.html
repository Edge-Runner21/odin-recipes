Pickled Onions

Description:

These crunchy pink pickled onions are quick and easy to make, and are great in salads, or on a burger.

They can be ready to eat after 2 hours or longer if you want the onions to be more pink.

Ingredients:

  • 2 red onions
  • 1 part water
  • 1 part vinegar
  • Salt (a big pinch)

Steps:

  1. Very thinly slice the red onions and add them to a jar.
  2. Add equal parts vinegar and water to a pot (enough to submerge the onions in the jar) with a big pinch of salt, and bring to a boil, then turn off the heat.
  3. Pour the hot pickling liquid over the onions until they are submerged or the jar is full.
  4. Cover and let sit in the fridge for at least 2 hours before serving.
  5. Enjoy!
