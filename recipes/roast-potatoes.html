Roast Potatoes

Description:

These crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside roast potatoes are perfect for a sunday roast, and are sure to delight friends and family.

Ingredients:

  • 1kg Maris Piper potatoes
  • 100g goose fat or 6tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp flour
  • sea salt flakes, to serve

Steps:

  1. Put a roasting tin in the oven and heat the oven to 200/fan 108c/gas 6.
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut each into 4 even-sized pieces or 2-3 if smaller.
  3. To a large pout add salt, potatoes and enough water to cover, and bring to a boil.
  4. Once boiling lower the and simmer for 2 minutes.
  5. While the potatoes are simmering add the goose fat or olive or to the roasting and let it heat for a few minutes.
  6. Once the potatoes have simmered, drain away all the water and toss them in the pot to fluff them up.
  7. Add the flour and toss again.
  8. Now add the potatoes to the roasting tin and coat them with the hot goose fat or olive oil.
  9. Put the potatoes in the oven and roast for 15 minutes, then take them out and turn them over. Repeat this 2 more times or until golden and crisp.
  10. Once cooked remove them from the oven, scatter with sea salt flakes and serve.
